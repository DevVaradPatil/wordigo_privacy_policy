Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21st, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wordigo ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application. By using Wordigo, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We design our app to minimize data collection. However, certain data is required for the app to function, display ads, and process purchases.

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version (Android/iOS), and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about your game progress, such as levels completed (Career/Zen mode), gems earned, and ad interactions to improve the game experience.
  • Crash Logs: If the app crashes, we collect log data to help us fix bugs.

Ads & In-App Purchases

Advertising

Wordigo uses Google AdMob to display ads. These services may use your device's Advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your Android settings under Settings > Google > Ads.

In-App Purchases

Our app allows you to purchase virtual items (e.g., Gems) via Google Play Billing. We do not process or store your financial information (credit card numbers) directly; all transactions are handled securely by Google.

Data Retention & Security

We retain your game progress locally on your device using shared preferences. If you use Firebase features, some data may be stored in the cloud to sync your progress. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.
